01.

Student BIM Program
The Foundations of BIM course is tailored for beginners seeking a comprehensive introduction to the world of Building Information Modeling. From understanding core concepts to hands-on exploration of essential tools, this course provides a solid base for your BIM journey. Dive into LOD 0 to 100, covering project initiation, basic modeling, and collaborative practices. This course sets the stage for a successful BIM career.

Total Hours: 6 Hours
Curriculum
Course 1 ( 3 Hours ): Modelling & Documentation
• BIM Project Setup.
• Creating a BIM model (Architectural, Structural and basics of MEP).
• Creating Construction drawings and publisher sets.
• Foundations of Clash detection and issue manager.
Course 2 (3 Hours): Visualization
• Types of 3D views forpresentation.
• Model View and quality check inArchicad BIM.
• Rendering in Archicad and with external software.

03.

BIM Excellence Program
This program is incredibly useful for architects looking to enhance their expertise in BIM and Archicad. It provides a deep understanding of advanced tools and techniques, enabling you to create high-quality, detailed architectural models. By covering all Levels of Development (LOD) from 0 to 500, it ensures you are proficient in every aspect of design and project management. The structured curriculum builds a solid foundation for future projects, making you more efficient, accurate, and competitive in the field.
